About #AGRI AND POULTRY TEC

Agri & Poultry Tec Expo 2026 stands as India’s most comprehensive business platform dedicated to the advancement of both agriculture and poultry sectors. The event brings global innovators, technology providers, equipment manufacturers, feed and nutrition suppliers, farm automation experts, and solution-driven enterprises together under one roof. It creates an unmatched environment for product showcases, knowledge exchange, and brand visibility at scale.

Designed to serve serious buyers and decision-makers from across India and international markets, the expo enables exhibitors to access new business channels, strike strategic partnerships, and expand their market presence. For visitors, it delivers hands-on exposure to groundbreaking technologies, economic solutions, and skill-enhancing expertise that drive measurable growth.
